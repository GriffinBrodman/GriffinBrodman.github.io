Maize

Actionscript, HaxeFlixel


Reklaw is a cursed snake just looking for friends! Help him stop the tourists trying to escape his corn maze, so he can keep them as friends forever.

I was asked to join a team doing an indepedent research project, making a video game with the games professor. On a team with three other programmers, two artists, and a music engineer.


As a programmer, I was given several tasks. I was responsible for implementing music, designing the snakes movement, implementing basic AI and pathfinding, and basic raycasting.
