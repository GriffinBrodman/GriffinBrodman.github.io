Beck & Chuck

Farseer, XNA


Chuck the Ball! Play as Beck, a young acrobatic rebel, as she fights her way through a factory, killing robots by throwing Chuck at them.



I am currently taking a Game Design class in college, and this is my group's semester long project.


Right now the project is very much a work in progress, updates will soon follow.
